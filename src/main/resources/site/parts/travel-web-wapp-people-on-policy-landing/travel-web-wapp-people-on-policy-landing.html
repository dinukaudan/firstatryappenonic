Add cover for your family and friends?

Tell us who else you’d like to be covered in your policy – up to 4 adults (18years+) and 4 children (0-17years).

Once they’re added onto your cover, you can choose which trips and activities they’re part of - so you only pay for their cover when it’s needed.

You can remove people from your policy later.
