Health statement

The quote we have offered you has made some assumptions about you and your circumstances.

In order to continue with your purchase and be covered by this policy, you must agree that, You;

  • are medically fit to travel
  • are not travelling against medical advice
  • are not travelling to seek medical advice, treatment or check-ups
  • are not awaiting any surgery or medical procedure
  • are not awaiting any tests or investigations, or the results of any tests, or investigations
  • do not currently have any undiagnosed symptoms
  • have not been given a terminal prognosis

It is also a condition of the cover provided, that you notify us about any change in your health, or the treatment of any of your medical conditions. If you do not advise us, then your cover may become invalid.
